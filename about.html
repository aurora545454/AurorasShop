🐾 Our Story

A tiny store with a big heart.

Hi. It's just me — a girl and her dog.

His name is Aurora. He's the reason this place exists.

A few years ago, I broke my back. It changed everything. The kind of work I used to do — standing, lifting, long hours — just wasn't possible anymore. I won't lie, it's been hard. Some days are harder than others. But through it all, I've had one constant: my dog, right by my side, tail wagging, reminding me that even on the tough days, there's still joy to be found.

We love going on walks together. But I kept running into the same problem — finding good quality things for him (and for myself) was exhausting. Endless scrolling, questionable products, things that looked great online but fell apart in a week. I got so fed up that I thought: why not make one place where I can gather everything I actually like?

So I did.

Auroras Store is my little corner of the internet. Everything here is something I've found, tried, and genuinely love. Things that make life a little easier, a little warmer, a little brighter — for me, for my dog, and maybe for you too.

I know I'm just one person. I can't compete with big stores or giant warehouses. But that's not what this is about. This is about sharing the good things I've found, one product at a time. And every time someone buys something from Auroras Store, it helps me keep going — it helps a single person live a bit better life.

So thank you. Whether you're here for your dog, for yourself, or just browsing — you're part of this little story now.

🐾 With love,
The girl behind Auroras Store
