How to place orders with suppliers for fulfillment

📌 AliExpress — How to Order

1. Click the supplier link

Each product has an AliExpress link. Click it to go to the product page.

2. Select the variant

Choose the correct color/size/version the customer ordered.

3. Enter customer's shipping address

Copy from PayPal notification. Make sure name, address, phone, and postal code are exact.

4. Select shipping method

Choose free shipping (ePacket or AliExpress Standard) to keep costs down.

5. Complete purchase

Pay the supplier cost using your own card. The supplier ships directly to your customer.

6. Send tracking to customer

Forward the tracking number from AliExpress to the customer's email so they can track their order.

💡 Pro Tips

  • ✅ Always message the supplier first to confirm they have stock
  • ✅ Order a sample of your best-selling products to check quality
  • ✅ Use ePacket shipping for fastest delivery (7-14 days vs 20-40)
  • ✅ Save screenshots of the order confirmation from AliExpress
  • ✅ Keep a spreadsheet of what you've ordered and tracking numbers
  • ❌ Don't use your store name as the return address — use a generic name
  • ❌ Don't tell customers you're dropshipping — say "our fulfillment partner"
